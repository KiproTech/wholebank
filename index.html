About WBYEF

The World Bank Youth Empowerment Funds (WBYEF) is a prestigious global initiative founded in 2025 through a landmark joint partnership between the World Bank and the United Nations. Our mandate is clear: to financially empower young people under the age of 35 by funding and supporting their entrepreneurial, agricultural, and development projects.

We believe that youth are the engine of tomorrow's economy. Through our program, we provide direct financial grants to young innovators, farmers, technologists, and entrepreneurs — helping them turn bold ideas into real, impactful ventures that uplift communities and nations.
